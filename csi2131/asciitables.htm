ASCII Tables for DOS and Unix

Here are the display characters corresponding to ASCII codes 0 to 255 in both DOS and Unix. Since the original ASCII standard only defined 7-bit ASCII codes, the characters above 127 (0x7F) are not standard from platform to platform. Codes less than 32 (0x20) and the code 127 (0x7F) are standard, but they were meant as control characters and never meant to be displayed. DOS decided to give them display characters anyway.
