New regulation concerning examinations

All exam conflicts are to be reported to your faculty, school or department A.S.A.P.  Exam conflicts, for students on an exchange program, are resolved by the student's home university. Misreading the exam schedule is not an excuse to miss the exam.

An exam date cannot be changed without approval from the faculty and the professor as well as a signed petition from all students registered in the course.
