Some things we need to remember about normalized and denormalized forms:

Normalized: We need our number to be in the form 1.f * 2e
Denormalized: We need our number in the form 0.f * 2-127


If you are unsure wether the number is in normalized or denormalized form, perform a check. Remember that the ranges for e are:

-128 < e < 128 for normalized
e < -127 for denormalized


The check is performed as follows:

1.f * 10i = 1.m * 2j
j = i/log 2


If j falls outside of the range of e for normalized form, we must use denormalized form.



Hint: It is a good idea to perform this check for small number, as it will save you a lot of time.
