Segmentation: comments
nIn each segment table entry we have both the starting address and length of the segment
uthe segment can thus dynamically grow or shrink as needed (but if they grow, they may need to be moved…)
uaddress validity easily checked with the length field
nBut variable length segments introduce external fragmentation and are more difficult to swap in and out...
nIt is natural to provide protection and sharing at the segment level since segments are visible to the programmer (pages are not)
nUseful protection bits in segment table entry:
uread-only/read-write bit
uSupervisor/User bit
