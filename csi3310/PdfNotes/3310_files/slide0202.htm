Solution of P/C: finite circular buffer of size k
Initialization: S.count:=1; //mutual excl.
                N.count:=0; //full spaces
                E.count:=k; //empty spaces
Producer:
repeat
  produce v;
  wait(E);
  wait(S);
  append(v);
  signal(S);
  signal(N);
forever
Consumer:
repeat
  wait(N);
  wait(S);
  w:=take();
  signal(S);
  signal(E);
  consume(w);
forever
critical sections
append(v):
b[in]:=v;
in++;
     mod k;
take():
w:=b[out];
out++;
      mod k;
return w;
