This assignment uses the supplier-part-project database listed on the reverse side. Create both the relational algebra and the tuple calculus expressions to answer the following nine (9) questions.

1.	Provide complete details of all suppliers in London.

2.	Provide project names and cities.

3.	Provide all cities in which a supplier or project (or both) are located.

4.	Provide supplier numbers for suppliers who supply part P1.

5.	Provide project names which use red or green parts.

6.	Provide the part names used in a project with quantity equal to 500.

7.	Provide all the cities in which suppliers are located, but no parts are located.

8.	Provide the supplier name/part name pairs for which the indicated supplier does not supply the indicated part.

9.	Provide the supplier name/part name pairs for which the indicated supplier does not supply the indicated part.
